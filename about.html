About

Dee Frost Jewellery Design

I live and work in beautiful Cornwall.

I have been a designer & maker for over ten years, studied jewellery and silversmithing, gaining a BA(hons).
I love using all kinds of material and have a special interest in re-using metals within my projects.

I have always recycled my own waste metals, and a larger portion of wares are made using environmentally friendly materials and methods.

As we all become aware of the need to save our resources to the best of our ability, I am delighted to be able to offer jewellery made with recycled ECO silver.
It's Environmentally friendly, produced using 100% recycled, scrapped metal.

ECO silver is produced using a totally traceable, fully audited process. It Has the same quality and working properties as sterling silver.

We can all agree, promoting recycled goods will create a greener world.
