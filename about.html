About

Dee Frost Jewellery Design

I live and work in beautiful Cornwall.

We have all become aware of the need to save our resources to the best of our ability.

Our jewellery is lovingly made with recycled ECO silver.
It's Environmentally friendly, produced using 100% recycled, scrapped metal.

Our waste metals, and a larger portion of wares are made using environmentally friendly materials and methods.

ECO silver is produced, using a totally traceable, fully audited process.

Eco silver Has the same quality and working properties as sterling silver.

We can all agree, promoting recycled goods will create a greener world.
